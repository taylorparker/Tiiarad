Meet our Radiologists

Experience the difference with subspecialty trained radiologists.

Interventional radiologists are board-certified physicians who utilize minimally invasive, image-guided procedures to diagnose and treat almost every organ system. Interventional radiology benefits the patient because it has less risk, less pain, and less recovery time than open surgery. Our IR team is well known for their expertise and has won awards for their innovative procedures. Check out Interventional Radiology Procedures for more details on specific procedures.
